covid-19 response plan

BGMCS is committed to providing families with the best experience possible while also implementing precautions to keep our community safe.

Health, Safety and Welfare

At BGMCS, the health, safety and welfare of all stakeholders who use are site is extremely important.As always, we put safety first to protect our students and staff. The health and safety of your child has never been more top-of-mind for you, and for us. Rest assured, when your child is in our program (pandemic or not) all BGMCS teachers and staff are trained in students care and will always look after your child. Our leadership team and governors works hard to ensure that having a safe environment is at the centre of everything we do.

Updated Health & Safety Procedures

During the COVID-19 pandemic, we continue to follow the latest guidance from medical advisors, state and local health departments. Our students and staff are required to wear a face mask that covers their mouth and nose.We have adapted our health and safety protocols and change these regularly in light of external factors.

If a student tests positive

Students who test positive for COVID-19 will not be able to return to school until instructed to do so by the Division of Public Health. parents should notify the school when it is safe for the student to return.

To learn more about what to expect at your location, contact your center director. The center director will be able to share the most current and accurate community-specific guidelines regarding daily drop-off and pick-up, masking, quarantining, and more.
