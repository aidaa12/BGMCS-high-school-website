About Us



Ten years ago, the Regional State Bureau of Education and an overwhelming number of Bahir Dar city residents appealed to the Bahir Dar Catholic Development Coordination Office to open a high school in the area, so that disadvantages children from the area could receive an education, in order to avoid a life of living on the street. The High School relies on the generosity of donors to pay the teacher’s salaries and school fees for most of the children. Some families are a position to pay a proportion of the school fees and this goes directly to the running costs of the school.

BGMCS High School offers extensive academic and extracurricular experiences to ensure high levels of learning for all students. BGMCS’ ability to offer such opportunities is supported in large part by the BGMCS staff and community. Located in the heart of BahirDar city, Ethiopia

The community’s diversity is reflected by BGMCS High School’s student population through a blend of ethnicities, cultures, socioeconomic and academic backgrounds. What is particularly impressive is the sense of community one feels when stepping onto the BGMCS campus. Providing college preparatory education for all students provides staff the welcome challenge of meeting these diverse needs.

At BGMCS More college preparatory boarding school, we strive to embody a full and diverse experience in order to best prepare our students for the things that matter. Diversity is promoted and celebrated as a core value that greatly enriches one's academic pursuits. We proudly welcome students committed to reaching their highest potential from all corners of the globe and walks of life. We also believe that all students should be empowered to reach their highest potential, despite any past hardships. Here, our students will learn an essential truth: the more you apply yourself, the more you can achieve.

Our Vision

To be the premier neighbourhood school of choice for students, parents and teachers. BGMCS students will have the opportunity to reach their true potential and be exceptionally well prepared for competitive postsecondary education and careers in the 21st century global community

Our Mission

To create a safe and supportive school environment for students that,

  • Integrates collaborative teaching and learning practices
  • Promotes self-regulation and skill building that is trauma sensitive
  • Facilities engaging, rigorous and individualised instructions
